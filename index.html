About

What?

DRISA is a steering wheel cover that measures biometrics and is the only preventative, one-time installation product on the market that monitors heart rate & skin conductance to alert drivers when fatigued via a bluetooth audio.

How?

The pulse sensors to measures the driver's heart rate. A gyroscope senses if the driver is turning the steering wheel or not. If the driver is fatigued, the case uses bluetooth audio to warn the driver.

Who?

It is suitable for every steering wheel. All drivers can use Drisa easily. Shift workers, drivers with sleep disorders, night-time travelers, new drivers...
